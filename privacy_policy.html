Privacy Policy - PES Stats Paste Extension

Effective Date: August 2025

Overview

PES Stats Paste (“the Extension”) is a browser add-on that allows users to paste player statistics from their clipboard into a compatible website. This Privacy Policy describes how the Extension handles user data.

Data Collection and Use

  • The Extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.
  • Clipboard access is only used locally when the user clicks the “Paste Stats” button.
  • The pasted content (e.g., player information) is processed entirely within the browser and is not sent to any external server.
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising technologies are integrated into the Extension.

Permissions

The Extension requests the following permissions:

  • clipboardRead: Required to read clipboard text after the user initiates a paste action.
  • scripting / content script access: Required to insert the “Paste Stats” button and fill form fields on the target website.

These permissions are used solely to enable the described functionality.

Data Sharing

  • The Extension does not share data with third parties.
  • No information is transmitted to the developer.

Security

Since all operations occur locally in the user's browser, there is no server-side component that stores or processes data.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the version published with the Extension.
